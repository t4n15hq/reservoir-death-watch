What this is

Satellite area is observed.
Storage and risk are derived.

01

Observe water area

Sentinel-2 measures current reservoir surface area. Sentinel-1 is the fallback when monsoon clouds make optical imagery unusable.

02

Anchor to CWC

CWC bulletins provide authoritative storage readings. Where a reservoir has no CWC calibration yet, the dashboard labels the storage estimate as a proxy.

03

Project depletion

A linear regression over recent surface-area observations projects days to dead storage under neutral and El Niño-suppressed monsoon scenarios.

04

Surface uncertainty

The dashboard shows stale data, missing fits, unreviewed AOIs, and backtest misses instead of smoothing them away. KRS 2023 remains the known hard case.
